PC Analysis, under the hood of library function

 Align all frames trajectory to the first. This step optional 
Construct a 3Nx3N  covariance matrix for the N atoms in your trajectory. The covariance is, by default, to the average structure over the trajectory, although you can provide a mean to replace it.
Diagonalize the covariance matrix. The eigenvectors are the principal components and the eigenvalues are the associated variance.
Sort the eigenvalues in descending order, so that the principal components are ordered by variance
